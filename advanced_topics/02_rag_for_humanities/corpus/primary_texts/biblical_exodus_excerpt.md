# Exodus-Inspired Passage

They left the house of bondage before they knew how to live as a free people.
The sea opened behind them, but fear traveled with them into the wilderness.
Liberation began with departure, but it did not end there.

## Notes

This invented passage is inspired by Exodus themes but is not a biblical translation.

Possible concepts include:

- liberation
- bondage
- transition
- wilderness
- freedom as process
