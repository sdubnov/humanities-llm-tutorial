# Uncertainty Policy

Metaphor annotations should include uncertainty when:

- multiple interpretations are plausible
- the source or target domain is unclear
- the device might be metaphor, simile, symbol, or personification
- the passage lacks wider context
- the interpretation depends on cultural or historical knowledge not provided

## Confidence Guidance

```text
1 = very uncertain
2 = weak or speculative
3 = plausible but incomplete
4 = strong but with some ambiguity
5 = very strong textual support
```

Do not assign confidence 5 if:

- alternative readings are substantial
- context is missing
- cultural or historical claims are involved
- the evidence is indirect
