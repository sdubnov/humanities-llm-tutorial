# Metaphor Taxonomy

## Metaphor

An implicit comparison where one domain is understood through another.

Required fields:

- evidence
- source domain
- target domain
- interpretation
- uncertainty note

## Simile

An explicit comparison, often using "like" or "as."

## Personification

Attributing human or animate action to a nonhuman entity.

## Symbol

An object, image, or figure that carries broader meaning beyond itself.

## Ambiguous Figurative Language

A passage may combine several devices.

When uncertain, record the ambiguity rather than forcing one category.
