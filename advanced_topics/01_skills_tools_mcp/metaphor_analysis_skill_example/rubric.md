# Metaphor Analysis Rubric

| Criterion | 1 | 3 | 5 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Evidence quality | Little or no exact evidence | Some relevant evidence | Exact and relevant textual evidence |
| Device accuracy | Misidentifies the device | Device is plausible but uncertain | Device is well justified |
| Source / target domains | Domains missing or confused | Domains partly clear | Domains clearly distinguished |
| Affect or tone | Missing or generic | Some tone identified | Tone is specific and text-supported |
| Interpretation | Vague or unsupported | Plausible but underdeveloped | Clear evidence-to-claim reasoning |
| Alternative reading | Missing | Present but weak | Meaningful alternative reading |
| Confidence | Arbitrary | Somewhat justified | Appropriate to evidence strength |
