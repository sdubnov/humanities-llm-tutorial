# Instructor Notes

## Why save markdown summaries?

LLM conversations are ephemeral.
Research memory should be persistent and inspectable.

## Why compare same-chat and restart?

This teaches:
- hidden context
- reproducibility
- context dependence

## How does an LLM become a research assistant?

- prompting
- structured prompting
- multi-turn interaction
- in-context learning
- teacher annotations
- fine-tuning

## Why use agents?

Role separation improves:
- critique
- revision
- methodological control
