# Student Assignment: Postmodern Vibe Agentic Matrixing

## Purpose

In this assignment, you will use role-based LLM agents to analyze a small corpus of postmodern literary texts. Your goal is not to prove that the LLM is “right.” Your goal is to build, run, and critique a transparent interpretive workflow.

## What you will do

1. Choose 5–8 literary texts from the provided corpus or another instructor-approved corpus.
2. Run each text through three analytical agents:
   - Ontological Tracker
   - Affective Vibe-Scout
   - Media Formalist
3. Record the scores, justifications, and reliability warnings in `corpus_template.csv`.
4. Run `run_analysis.py` on your completed CSV file.
5. Write a 500–800 word methodological reflection.

## Required submission

Submit:

- your completed matrix CSV;
- one revised agent prompt;
- the script-generated report or equivalent manually created report;
- your methodological reflection.

## Reflection questions

Your reflection should answer at least four of the following:

1. Which agent produced the most useful interpretation?
2. Which agent drifted away from its assigned role?
3. Which score seemed most falsely precise?
4. Where did the model rely on reputation rather than evidence?
5. What did the matrix reveal that an ordinary paragraph response might hide?
6. What did the matrix flatten or distort?
7. How would your results change if you used full passages instead of summaries?
8. What fourth agent would you add, and why?

## Grading emphasis

You are graded on the quality of your method and critique, not on whether your scores match the instructor's opinions.
