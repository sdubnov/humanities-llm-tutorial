# Metaphor Analysis Examples

## Example 1

Text:

```text
The city was a broken mirror, reflecting every dream I had lost.
```

Analysis:

- device: metaphor
- evidence: "The city was a broken mirror"
- source domain: broken mirror
- target domain: city
- affect: loss, fragmentation
- interpretation: the city reflects damaged memory or fractured aspiration
- alternative reading: the mirror may suggest self-recognition
- confidence: 5
