# Failure Modes

## Theme/device confusion

The model may call a general theme a metaphor even when no domain mapping is present.

## Unsupported cultural claim

The model may infer historical, gender, racial, or political meaning without textual evidence.

## Flattened ambiguity

The model may choose one interpretation too quickly.

## Generic analysis

The model may produce vague commentary that could apply to many texts.

## Missing evidence

The model may explain without quoting.
