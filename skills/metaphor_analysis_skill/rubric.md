# Metaphor Analysis Rubric

Score each output from 1–5.

## Evidence

Does the output quote exact textual evidence?

## Category fit

Is the device correctly classified?

## Source/target mapping

Are source and target domains clear?

## Interpretation

Is the interpretation plausible and supported?

## Ambiguity

Does the output acknowledge alternative readings?

## Overinterpretation

Does the output avoid unsupported claims?
